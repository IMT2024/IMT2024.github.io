Tournament Information

All the details about the Igiehon Mathematics Tournament 2024.

Tournament Structure

Stage Description
Initial Round A 50-question objective exam administered to all participants. The top eight scorers qualify their schools for the finals. If two students from the same school are in the top eight, the next highest-scoring student qualifies their school.
Finals The top eight schools compete in teams of two. Teams are divided into two groups based on their rankings. Group winners advance to the semi-finals.
Semi-Finals A single-elimination round for the top four teams, leading to the final and third-place match.
Finals The final match determines the overall winner, and a third-place match determines the runner-up.

Prizes

  • 1st Place: ₦1,000,000
  • 2nd Place: ₦750,000
  • 3rd Place: ₦500,000
  • Top 3 Students: Laptops
  • Top 10 Students, Teachers, and Schools: Consolation prizes
