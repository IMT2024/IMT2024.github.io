Mission statement

The Igiehon Mathematics Tournament is committed to igniting a passion for mathematics in young minds by providing a challenging, engaging, and collaborative platform for students to showcase their talents. Our mission is to inspire the next generation of problem solvers, critical thinkers, and innovators, fostering a culture of academic excellence, perseverance, and lifelong learning. We believe that through competition and teamwork, students can unlock their full potential, transforming their futures and contributing to the advancement of society through the power of mathematics.

Resources for Math Enthusiasts

Art of Problem Solving (AoPS)

A platform providing resources, textbooks, and online courses for serious math students.

Wolfram Alpha

A computational search engine that provides answers to math problems and offers step-by-step solutions.

MIT OpenCourseWare

A free collection of MIT course materials, including mathematics courses at all levels.

Maths4Life

Math4Life Foundation is dedicated to promoting math education and empowering students to excel in mathematics.

Mathway

An online problem solver that helps students understand and solve math problems step-by-step.

Project Euler

A collection of challenging math and computational problems needing more than just math skills to solve.
