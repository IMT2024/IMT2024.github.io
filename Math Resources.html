Math Resources and Competitions for Secondary School Students

Our Mission

The Igiehon Mathematics Tournament aims to foster mathematical excellence and inspire students by providing them with the opportunity to explore their full potential, collaborate with peers, and pursue careers in STEM. We believe in building the next generation of problem solvers, critical thinkers, and innovators.
